# Privacy Policy for LookAlike App

**Last Updated:** December 18, 2025

## Introduction

LookAlike ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our app users ("you", "your"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Data
LookAlike **does not collect, store, or transmit personal information** about you. All image processing and facial analysis happens locally on your device.

### Photos and Images
- Photos you select for comparison are processed **entirely on your device**
- Photos are **never uploaded** to our servers or any third-party services
- Photos are stored locally only for the duration of your session
- You can delete photos at any time by closing the app

### Usage Data Collected by Third Parties
We use third-party services that may collect information to provide their services:

**Google AdMob (Advertising)**
- Device information (model, OS version)
- Ad interaction data
- Approximate location (based on IP address)
- Advertising identifier (IDFA/AAID)

Purpose: To show you personalized advertisements.
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Google Play Billing**
- Purchase information for premium features
- Transaction history

Purpose: To process in-app purchases.
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## How We Use Information

We **do not** use your information. Third-party services use data as described in their respective privacy policies:
- **AdMob**: To display personalized ads and measure ad performance
- **Google Play Billing**: To process and manage your purchases

## Your Rights (GDPR/RGPD)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

1. **Right to Access**: Request information about data collected
2. **Right to Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
3. **Right to Object**: Object to data processing for advertising purposes
4. **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Change your consent preferences at any time

### Managing Ad Personalization
You can manage your advertising preferences:
- **In the app**: Go to Privacy Settings (when implemented)
- **Android Settings**: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **AdMob Consent**: We show a consent dialog on first launch in the EEA

## Data Security

- All facial analysis is performed locally on your device
- Photos are not transmitted over the internet
- We implement industry-standard security measures
- Third-party services (AdMob, Google Play) use their own security protocols

## Children's Privacy

LookAlike is a family-friendly app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian discovers that their child has provided personal information without consent, please contact us.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Showing a notice in the app (for major changes)
